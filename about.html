Meet Our Founder: Alex Kifaru

As the visionary behind Kifaru Tours, Alex has dedicated over 15 years to African wildlife conservation and tourism.

Key Projects

  • Rhino Conservation Initiative (2018-Present): Led efforts to protect black rhinos in Tanzania, partnering with local communities to reduce poaching by 40%.
  • Great Migration Documentary (2022): Produced an award-winning film highlighting wildebeest migrations, raising awareness for sustainable tourism.
  • Cultural Exchange Program (2020): Developed tours integrating Maasai traditions, supporting education in remote villages.
  • Eco-Lodge Development (2015): Built sustainable lodges in Serengeti, emphasizing low-impact design and local employment.

These projects reflect our commitment to ethical adventures.
